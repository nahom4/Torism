Top three activities to do at Lalibela

Bete giorgis
The most famous of the 11 rock hewn churches of Lalibela is a fascinating work of art and religion. Built in the 10th century BC this church which was chisled from solid rock is the biggest sclupture in the world. belived to have been built by king Lalibela with the assistance of angles it commands a great respect and admiration in the ethiopian orthodox religion.

Ben Abeba
A resturant that looks like it's straight out of a scifi-movie,the Ben Abeba,is a very good place to relax and have delicious meals.

Hicking
Lalibela has a great land scape for hicking, a great number of wild animals that will fill anyone with a sense of awe and further more it has a very beautifull and hospitable community.
